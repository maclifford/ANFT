# About Us Section — Integration Instructions

## What this addon contains (all NEW files — nothing overwrites your edits)
- 6 pages: who-we-are, history-and-lineage, our-trainers, our-partners,
  student-testimonials, media-and-blog (.html)
- images/: 5 new banner images for those pages
- images/team/: folder for team portraits (see its README for filenames)

## How to integrate (5 minutes)
1. Copy everything in this addon INTO your site folder, merging the images/
   folders. Nothing here shares a name with your existing files.
2. Open Claude Code in your site folder and paste this prompt:

   "Six new About pages were just added (who-we-are.html and its five
   siblings). Integrate them:
    1. Add an 'About' link pointing to who-we-are.html in the header nav
       of every existing page, placed after 'A Living System'.
    2. Make the header and footer of the six new pages exactly match the
       header and footer of membership.html, so any recent logo or footer
       edits I've made carry over.
    3. Add the six new pages to sitemap.xml.
    4. Verify every internal link on the six new pages resolves to a real
       file in this folder, and report anything broken.
    5. If our header logo or hub symbol filenames have changed from
       anft-logo-horizontal.webp / hub-symbol.webp, update the new pages
       to match."

3. Refresh index.html in your browser: the About link should appear, and
   who-we-are.html should show the team grid (monogram circles until
   photos are added to images/team/).

## Content notes
- History timeline years 2016-2026 were inferred from context; verify
  against your records.
- Testimonials page deliberately contains only the one verified quote —
  the staging site's testimonials included fabricated names. Gather real,
  permissioned quotes before expanding it.
