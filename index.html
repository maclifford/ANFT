The Forest Threshold

In residence with the land. The Threshold is complete in itself — its five movements are Connect, Discover, Embody, Remember, and Shine — and from it, five streams flow — four academies, and Nature as Medicine, a partnership program for health care professionals. The five paths, in plain terms

  • Relational Forest Therapy Academy

    Become a certified forest therapy guide through a mentored, cohort-based practicum — and join a global community of practicing guides.

    Guide training · Curriculum development · Global guide map

  • Nature as Medicine

    Training for physicians and other health care professionals in nature-based approaches to health and healing, offered in partnership with Dr. Susan Abookirie of Harvard Medical School.

    About the program

  • Academy of Place

    Training for place-based stewards and certified trail consultants — shaping, certifying, and tending the places that hold this work.

    Consultant training · Trail certification · Global trail map

  • Opus Academy

    The Alchimage process, practices, and atelier — a creative-transformative path of assemblage and pilgrimage for your life and work.

    Assemblage · Process · Practices · Atelier · Pilgrimage

  • Academy of Applied Partnerships

    Propose and deliver your own offering through the academy — workshops, retreats, and specialized professional training.

    Workshops · Retreats · Specialized training · ANFT specialized training

The land itself is the elder here and everywhere in our world. From our home in Prescott, Arizona, we work on land that has hosted many lives across its long existence — human and other-than-human, some now gone from the Earth — and many cultures, most recently, before those of us here now, the Yavapai and Apache peoples and their relations. We honor the land, and all whom it has held, and its infinite threads of relational connections, in which we and our work are embedded, which we live into with awe and love, and for which we are profoundly grateful.
