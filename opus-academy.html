A Stream from the Source

Opus Academy

Purpose and emergence: a creative-transformative path for your life and work.

Opus is the stream for what wants to be made. Through assemblage — the gathering of found and given materials into meaning — and through the Alchimage process, practices, and atelier, participants work at the threshold between the inner life and the material world. Pilgrimage carries the practice out onto the land itself.

Where the other streams train you to guide others, Opus turns the practice toward your own becoming: the work of art and the work of a life, made in relationship with the living world.

A fuller presentation of Opus Academy — its offerings, teachers, and calendar — is being prepared. The application is open now, and the admissions circle welcomes early inquiries.

The land itself is the elder here and everywhere in our world. From our home in Prescott, Arizona, we work on land that has hosted many lives across its long existence — human and other-than-human, some now gone from the Earth — and many cultures, most recently, before those of us here now, the Yavapai and Apache peoples and their relations. We honor the land, and all whom it has held, and its infinite threads of relational connections, in which we and our work are embedded, which we live into with awe and love, and for which we are profoundly grateful.
