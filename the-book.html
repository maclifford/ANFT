The Story Behind the Book

The Main Guide

Your Guide to Forest Bathing (Expanded Edition): Experience the Healing Power of Nature

M. Amos Clifford

The bestselling guide to forest bathing — and the foundational text of a global movement. Simply being present in the natural world, with all our senses fully alive, can have a remarkably healing effect and awaken our profound connection with all living things. This expanded edition includes hands-on practices and space for journal entries and reflections.

The Journal

Your Guide to Forest Bathing — A Journal: Invitations for Practice and Reflection

M. Amos Clifford

The perfect companion to the guide — fifty-two invitations for practice and introspection, with space to record your experiences, thoughts, and emotions as you engage in forest bathing. Whether you’re just beginning or already rooted in the process, this journal supports you in finding presence and healing through regular practice.

The land itself is the elder here and everywhere in our world. From our home in Prescott, Arizona, we work on land that has hosted many lives across its long existence — human and other-than-human, some now gone from the Earth — and many cultures, most recently, before those of us here now, the Yavapai and Apache peoples and their relations. We honor the land, and all whom it has held, and its infinite threads of relational connections, in which we and our work are embedded, which we live into with awe and love, and for which we are profoundly grateful.
